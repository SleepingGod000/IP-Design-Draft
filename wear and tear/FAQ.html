Frequently Asked Questions(F.A.Q)



Who is this website meant for?

This website is meant for cyclist from beginner to veteral level.
The forum is to let cyclists share their cycling experiences that they have encountered in their lives.
The shop is a marketplace where people can buy bicycle related stuff there.


Who can sell on the marketplace?

Currently only authorised sellers can sell on the marketplace.
If a user want to become an authorised seller, they would need to apply by sending an email to our team with the required documentation as proof.


How can I as a user, sell off my 2nd hand items or unwanted new items?

A user can only sell 2nd hand items or unwanted new items.
However their listings will be limited to only 20 listings and will be immediately tagged as "2nd hand".


*If I am not satisfied with my purchase, can I return the items?

Items sold are final and not returnable.
However, if you even encounter problems with the items purchased, do not hesitate to contact the seller for further assistance.


What happens if I found a listing that contains counterfeit products?

Do not hesitate to report that listing.
We take stern action against listing containing counterfeit products.
If the user is found to be involved in such activities, their account will be immediately banned.


What can I do if I found a listing that does not sell products but instead is marketing their services?

Do not hesitate to report that listing.
We take stern action against listing which is aimed towards publicity/marketing of their services.
If the user is found to be involved in such activities, their account will be immediately banned.


I can only select the maximum option of purchasing 10 units of an item per transaction? How can I purchase more than 10 units for that particular item?

We limit the system to only handle 10 units of an item per transaction because in such scenarios, directly contacting the seller would result in a better overall deal for you as the consumer.
